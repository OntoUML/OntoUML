Definition
----------

«:ref:`material`» relation can be completely **derived** (via «:ref:`derivation`») from the «:ref:`relator`» and the corresponding «:ref:`mediation`» relations. Derivation makes the cardinality constraints of the mediation relations collapse (see «:ref:`material`» relation, example 2).

Also, several «:ref:`material`» relations can be derived from a single «:ref:`relator`» and «:ref:`mediation`» relations (see «:ref:`material`» relation, example 1).
